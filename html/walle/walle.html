WALL·E Demos

There are two short demonstrations of my Wall-e in action from the YouTube (images link to the videos).
Bath - is an example run of "get my wife out of the bath" program (3 minutes long). To capture the video instead of operating in the late night environment I simulate the night conditions covering the night detector (see a black tape on the Wall-e's back).
Second video - a short talk example between me and my Wall-e robot (25 seconds long).
