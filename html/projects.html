WALL·E Inspired Robot

I have been working upon that project for 10 years, mainly weekends when I found any free one. In April 2008 I bought LPC2378 Development Board but decision to use it for robot development I took after vacation in 2008 inspired by the Pixar's computer animated film. My motivation was to prove myself that after years of management type of work I am still able to write software. I wanted also to inspire my two sons (11 and 7 years old at that time) towards embedded systems. This I failed . None of them (at least up to now) took that path. Maybe I also wanted them to have a cool father(?) there is not too many who builds robots and WALL·E like in particular. I stopped the work after 10 years once I decided to work upon something different.

There is always problem to find out real application for a robot like this (I mean for home toy type of robots). In my case I decided my WALL·E main purpose will be to call my wife out of bath when she is there for too long at late night - quite a challenging and motivating task.

BBK GitHub Web Page

I have been thinking to publish my Wall-e project on the Web for a quite some time. There was always something what made me busy finally I decided to recall my HTML, CSS and JavaScript knowledge to setup something. I had a quite long term break in Web based technology so I first had to remind myself the things and also learn new ones (like responsive web design) because technology changed since the time when I learnt HTML first time. There was great source of the Web related knowledge I used in the past (w3schools.com) which is also right now in a quite impressive shape. Inspired by them and using their great templates I decided finally to develope static web page on GitHub to have it as a front page to what I am doing there.
